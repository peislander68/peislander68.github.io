About Me

Writer, Actor, Voice-Over Artist.

As a professional writer, editor, voice over artist, and actor, what drives me is communicating ideas through story. As artists we are living in exciting and challenging times. Overused traditional story structures and tropes are being broken and upcoming creatives are finding new ground in the ways we communicate story. The goal, I believe, is staying relevant with these new trends; and at the same time, effectively communicating universal themes of our human journey to an ever changing, multiculturally diverse, audience.
